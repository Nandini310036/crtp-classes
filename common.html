Common Questions

*Tell me about yourself:Short summary of your professional background.

*Why do you want this job?:Align your goals with the company mission.

*What are your strengths?: Pick 2-3 and back with examples.

*What is your biggest weakness?:Share one and show how you're improving it.

*Why should we hire you?:Match your experience to the job description.

*Where do you see yourself in 5 years?:Show ambition, but keep it relevant to the role/company.

*How do you handle pressure or tight deadlines?:Describe techniques you use to stay organized and calm.
